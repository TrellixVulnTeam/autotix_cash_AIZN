Add a new show
  1. Click Shows in the menu on the top of the page.
  2. Click Add a new show.
  3. Select a show from the drop-down menu.
  4. Click Add.
Add cash spent
  1. Click Shows in the menu on the top of the page.
  2. Click on a show. If there are no shows, follow the instructions above to add one.
  3. Click Add a new amount.
  4. Enter the amount. Enter digits only.
  5. Click Add.
